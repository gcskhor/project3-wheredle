So you think Singapore is boring?
You're probably right.
Wheredle helps you decide where to go on your next day out, in a fun and exciting way!
How to play
Guess the location in 5 tries to win.
Your guess needs to be from the options provided in the guess input.
After each guess, you will be given a distance and a direction towards the correct location.
Example
ArtScience Museum
2.15 km
The correct location is 2.15 km away from and Northeast of ArtScience Museum.
